## 2. THE PROBLEM (1 minute)

### Filipino Elementary Students & Geometry Struggles

**Key Statistics & Research:**
- **DepEd Studies (2019-2023)**: Mathematics consistently ranks as one of the lowest-performing subjects in National Achievement Tests, with geometry concepts showing particularly poor comprehension rates
- **TIMSS 2019 Data**: Filipino Grade 4 students scored below international average in geometry and measurement domains
- **Classroom Reality**: Traditional textbook-based geometry teaching lacks visual and interactive elements critical for spatial reasoning development

**Specific Pain Points:**
1. **Abstract Concepts**: Students struggle to visualize 2D and 3D shapes
2. **Limited Engagement**: Passive learning methods fail to maintain student interest
3. **No Practice Feedback**: Students don't know what they're doing wrong until tests
4. **One-Size-Fits-All**: No personalized learning paths for different skill levels
5. **Assessment Anxiety**: High-stakes testing without formative practice opportunities

**Target Demographics:**
- Primary: Grade 6 students (11-12 years old)
- Secondary: Elementary mathematics teachers
- Tertiary: Parents seeking supplemental learning tools

---

## 3. THE SOLUTION: Polegion (2 minutes)

### What is Polegion?
*"Polegion transforms geometry education into an interactive adventure game where students progress through magical castle realms, each teaching essential geometric concepts aligned with DepEd's K-12 curriculum."*

### Core Features:

#### **7 Progressive Worlds**
- **2 Assessment Castles** (Castle 0 & 6): Pre-test and post-test evaluations
- **5 Learning Castles** (Castles 1-5): Curriculum-based content
  - Castle 1: Plane & Solid Figures
  - Castle 2: Lines, Angles & Angle Relationships
  - Castle 3: Circles (Circumference & Area)
  - Castle 4: Polygons (Perimeter & Area)
  - Castle 5: Advanced Geometry Concepts

#### **Interactive Learning System**
- **Chapter-Based Lessons**: 4 chapters per castle with:
  - Animated tutorials
  - Interactive minigames
  - Immediate feedback
  - Visual demonstrations
- **Gamification Elements**:
  - Collect relics as achievement rewards
  - Unlock new castles progressively
  - Track personal progress visually
  - Earn XP and level up

#### **Collaborative Features**
- **Virtual Classrooms**: Teachers create rooms for their students
- **Competition Mode**: Real-time geometry challenges
- **Peer Learning**: Students can compete and learn together
- **Teacher Dashboard**: Monitor student progress and identify struggling concepts

#### **Assessment Integration**
- Pre-test (Castle 0) establishes baseline
- Post-test (Castle 6) measures improvement
- Adaptive difficulty based on performance
- Detailed analytics for teachers and parents
